About Me

My interest in data science and analytics began with my passionate following of the Golden State Warriors and the Seattle Seahawks, which translated into 3 straight successful fantasy basketball seasons. I'm also really interested in gardening and collecting rare plants from all over the world. In my free time, you can find me hooping, watching sports, binging The Boys or Family Guy, and hanging out with my family and friends.
